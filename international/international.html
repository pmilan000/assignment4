Venice, Italy

"Venice is one of the most interesting and beautiful places in Italy. You see a canal walking down almost every street, with the gondalas going here and there. The Piazza San Marco is sprawling and magnificent.

Orvieto, Italy

"When we went on our trip to Italy this was small town where our friends were getting married. (yes, another wedding overseas!) Orvieto has a cathedral, or Duomo as it is called, with a beautiful facade depicting the creation to the last judgement. There are 1200 underground tunnels you can explore under the town. Once you arrive there you can park your car and ride the funicular (which is the basically a cable train) up to the town."

Riviera Maya, Mexico

"We've actually visited here a few times. However the most recent trip was for my 40th birthday. We booked a trip to swim with Whale Sharks, which was an amazing life experience."

India

"Another wedding!! This time it was my niece that was getting married to an Indian man with family still living in India. We visited several cities with my favorites being Agra, to see the Taj Mahal; Jaipur, where we were able to visit with Elephants (which was amazing); and last but not least Udaipur, which is where they held the wedding festivies."
